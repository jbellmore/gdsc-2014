Guardian's First Annual Software Developer Conference!

7 Hanover Sq. NYC (Home Office) - Wednesday May 7th & Thursday May 8th

This conference is intended to promote best practices that are specific to developing and delivering a reliable and rich user experience across the application portfolio of Guardian Life. We hope to foster wide reaching peer communication and collaboration that will produce effieciencies captured by avoiding duplicate efforts, leveraging existing work products, and sharing lessons learned.

Participation is open to all IT roles at all levels.

All GL software systems professionals welcome, COTS integration folks too. This is not a technology stack specific event.

Come join the discussion about the conference in the First Annual Software Developer Conference group in Yammer!

Speakers

The Guardian technology conference features speakers from within various organizations within the Guardian enterprise as well as other industry experts.

Sessions

Sessions will cover a wide range of technology topics that would be of interest for all Guardian IT professionals. Speakers will cover topics that pertain to developing applications within the Guardian environment.

The conference will take place on the 15th floor of the Guardian home office at Hannover Square - New York, NY. The date for the conference will be May 7th & 8th.
