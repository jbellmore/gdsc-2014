Guardian Life 1st Annual Technology Conference

7 Hanover NYC (HO) Wednesday May 7th & Thursday May 8th

This conference is intended to promote best practices that are specific to developing and delivering a reliable and rich user experience across the application portfolio of Guardian Life. We hope to foster wide reaching peer communication and collaboration that will produce the effieciencies captured by avoiding duplicate efforts, leveraging existing work products, and sharing lessons learned.

All GL software systems professionals welcome, COTS integration folks too. This is not a technology stack specific event.
